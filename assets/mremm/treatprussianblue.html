Prussian blue

Consult latest REMM online version for current details.

Administered for Isotopes: Cesium-137, Thallium-201
Route of Administration & Dosage:
Adults: 0.5 g insoluble Prussian blue per capsule

  • 1 - 3 g tid with 100-200 mL water, up to 10-12 g/d (based on Goiânia accident data)
  • 3 g PO tid (see FDA drug label)
Children: 0.5 g insoluble Prussian blue per capsule
  • Age 2-12 years: 1 g PO tid
  • Capsules may be opened and mixed with food
  • FDA drug label contains pediatric information
  • Age < 2 years: not FDA approved (IND or EUA may be required)

Duration: 3 weeks, titrated by urine and fecal bioassay and whole-body counting
Mechanism of Action: Ion exchanger
Note: FDA approved for this indication
