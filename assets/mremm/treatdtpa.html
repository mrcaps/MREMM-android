Ca-DTPA, Zn-DTPA

Consult latest REMM online version for current details.

Administered for Isotopes: Plutonium-239, Americium-241, Curium-244
Route of Administration & Dosage:
IV: 1 g in 250 mL NS or 5% glucose, given in 1-2 h, or bolus over 3-4 min; given once daily as a single infusion
Nebulized Inhalation: 1 g in 1 : 1 dilution with water or NS over 15-20 min
Duration: Up to 5 days
Mechanism of Action: Chelating agent
Note: It has been approved by FDA for treating plutonium, americium and curium.
