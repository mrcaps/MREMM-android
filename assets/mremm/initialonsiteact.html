Major Goals of the Initial Response

  • Notify local authorities.
  • Establish local on-site Incident Command System
  • Establish perimeter security.
  • Ensure safety of responders
  • Identify specifics of the radiation threat, (which may not be apparent during the event discovery).
    • Measure radiation levels in the environment
    • Identify radioisotope(s) in the environment
  • Notify other appropriate (local, state, federal) authorities that a radiation event may have/has occurred
  • Identify approximate number and location of casualties
  • Protect the public from further injury by implementing Protective Actions and Protective Action Guides appropriate for the incident.
  • Begin triage of patients using protocols appropriate for the specific radiation event
  • Alert local medical infrastructure to prepare for mass casualty transports
