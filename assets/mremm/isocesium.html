Cesium-137

Symbol: Cs-137
Ionizing Radiation Type: β, γ
Radiological half-life: 30 years
Biologic half-life (days): 70
Exposure Type: External, Internal
Mode of Contamination: Lungs, GI tract, wounds, follows potassium
Focal Accumulation in Body: Renal excretion
Treatment (FDA approved for this indication): Ion exchange with Prussian blue
