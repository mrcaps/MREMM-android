Neurological Assessment
A - Alert
V - Responds to verbal stimuli
P - Responds to painful stimuli

  • Appropriate - e.g., withdrawal from painful stimulus
  • Inappropriate - e.g., posturing
U - Unresponsive to noxious stimuli
