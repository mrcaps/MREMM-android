Radiological vs. Nuclear Event

  • Radiological event
    • Does not involve a nuclear explosion (fission)
    • Victim can have contamination and/or exposure
    • Examples:
      • Radiological Dispersal Device (RDD): explosive/non-explosive
      • Hidden radioactive source: radiological exposure device (RED)
      • Nuclear reactor accident
      • Transportation accident
  • Nuclear event (blast, detonation, explosion)
    • Involves a nuclear explosion (fission)
    • Victims can have contamination and/or exposure
    • Examples:
      • Nuclear weapon
      • Improvised Nuclear Device
