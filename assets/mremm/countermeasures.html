Countermeasures for Treatment

Only the 3 FDA-approved countermeasures for internal contamination are listed on Mobile REMM. See REMM online for more detailed countermeasure information.

Countermeasures for treating internal contamination
  • Ca-DTPA, Zn-DTPA
  • Potassium iodide
  • Prussian blue

Hematopoietic countermeasures
  • G-CSF (filgrastim or Neupogen)
  • Pegylated G-CSF (pegfilgrastim or Neulasta)
  • GM-CSF (sargramostim or Leukine)
